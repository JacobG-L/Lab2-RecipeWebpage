Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken Pasta

Recipe Overview

This dish is a comfort‑food classic that I first made during a busy week when I needed something quick, filling, and flavorful. The creamy garlic sauce comes together in minutes, and the combination of tender chicken and parmesan makes it feel like a restaurant meal without the effort. It’s perfect for weeknights, meal prep, or impressing guests with minimal stress.

Ingredients

For the Chicken

  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

For the Pasta & Sauce

  • 300g fettuccine or linguine
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ cup reserved pasta water
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Instructions

  1. Slice the chicken breasts into thin strips.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  4. Cook the chicken for 5–7 minutes until golden and fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
  5. In the same pan, melt the butter.
  6. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
  7. Pour in the heavy cream and stir gently.
  8. Add parmesan cheese and mix until smooth.
  9. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  10. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  11. Reserve ½ cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta.
  12. Add the pasta to the sauce and toss to coat.
  13. Add pasta water as needed to thin the sauce.
  14. Return the cooked chicken to the pan and mix everything together.
  15. Garnish with parsley and serve warm.
